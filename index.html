How is it calculated?

Base Damage = (1 + 0.5 × C) × (1 + WD + 1.25 × SL) × (1 + 1.3 × SPL)
- WD: Weapon Damage
- SL: Sharpness Level
- C: Critical Hit (0 or 1)
- SPL: Strength Potion Level
Damage Reduction = (D - B × (D - 1) / 2) × (1 - 0.04 × A) × (1 - 0.04 × P) × (1 - 0.2 × R)
- D: Base Damage
- B: Blocking (0 or 1)
- A: Armor Points
- P: Total Protection Enchantment Level
- R: Resistance Effect Level
